## How I Work as an AI Agent

### AI Agent Architecture

As an AI agent, I'm designed to perceive, process information, and respond in a way that achieves specific goals. My architecture consists of several key components:

1. **Perception/Input**: I receive your prompts and questions as input, which I process to understand your intent.

2. **Memory**: I have two types of memory:
   - **Working memory**: Short-term context from our current conversation
   - **Persistent memory**: Knowledge from my training data that I can access when needed

3. **Planning Module**: I analyze your request and determine the best approach to respond. This involves breaking down complex questions into manageable parts.

4. **Execution Layer**: I generate my response based on the plan, drawing on my knowledge and reasoning capabilities.

5. **Feedback Loop**: While I don't learn from individual conversations, my responses are based on patterns from my training data.

### Types of AI Agents

There are three main types of AI agents:

1. **Reactive agents**: Respond immediately to inputs without memory or planning
2. **Deliberative agents**: Build a world model and plan before taking action
3. **Hybrid agents** (like me): Combine reactive speed with deliberative planning

As a hybrid agent, I can quickly respond to your questions while also considering broader context and reasoning through complex problems.
